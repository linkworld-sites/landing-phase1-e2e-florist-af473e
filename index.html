Why Petal & Keep

Beauty That Endures Every Season

We believe flowers shouldn't be fleeting. Every bloom in our collection is thoughtfully sourced, gently dried, and arranged to honour the natural imperfections that make each piece uniquely yours.

🌾

Artisan-Dried Botanicals

Every stem is slow-dried using traditional methods — no chemicals, no shortcuts. The result is vivid colour retention that lasts for years, not weeks.

📦

Curated Monthly Boxes

Our seasonal subscription boxes arrive with hand-selected varieties, a styling card, and a vase recommendation — everything you need to create a statement display.

💐

Bespoke Arrangements

Working on a wedding, event, or gifting project? Our floralists craft custom dried bouquets and installations tailored to your palette and vision.

♻️

Zero-Waste Packaging

All packaging is recycled, recyclable, or compostable. Our tissue, wrapping paper, and boxes are FSC-certified and printed with plant-based inks.

🌍

European Small Farms

We partner with independent growers across the Netherlands, France, and the UK — supporting biodiversity and fair practices at every step.

✂️

Styling Guides Included

Each order arrives with a beautifully printed care and styling guide — tips on arrangement, room placement, and how to refresh your display each season.

From Farm to Your Favourite Shelf

01

We Source Seasonally

Flowers are harvested at peak bloom from our partner farms across Europe, selected for their drying qualities and natural beauty.

02

Slow-Dried With Care

Bundles are hung in airy drying rooms for 3–6 weeks — no artificial heat, no chemicals. Just time and patience.

03

Curated & Arranged

Our in-house team hand-selects and arranges each bouquet, subscription box, or bespoke order with an eye for texture and colour harmony.

04

Delivered to You

Wrapped in recycled paper and nestled in a zero-waste box, your order arrives ready to display — no water, no fuss, just beauty.
